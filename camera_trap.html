Camera Trap Monitoring

This page provides resources and information about using machine learning for camera trap monitoring. Camera traps are motion-triggered cameras used to capture images of wildlife, providing valuable data on species presence, behavior, and population dynamics.

Esta página proporciona recursos e información sobre el uso del aprendizaje automático para el monitoreo con cámaras trampa. Las cámaras trampa son cámaras activadas por movimiento que se utilizan para capturar imágenes de vida silvestre, proporcionando datos valiosos sobre la presencia de especies, comportamiento y dinámica de poblaciones.

Summary

Camera traps are a powerful tool for wildlife monitoring, but they can be challenging to use due to the high volume of images they produce. Machine learning models can help automate the process of identifying and classifying images, allowing researchers to focus on the most important data.
